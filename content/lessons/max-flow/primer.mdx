### Primer: building intuition

Flows satisfy conservation constraints and capacity bounds. We illustrate with a small network and animate augmenting paths.

> _Key idea:_ every augmenting path increases flow until no residual capacity remains.

Inline math example: $f(u,v) \\le c(u,v)$.
